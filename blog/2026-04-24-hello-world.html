Hello World — Why I Started This Blog

April 24, 2026 · Chen Luo

I've been meaning to start writing for a long time. After over a decade in research and industry — from Rice to NEC Labs to Amazon Search — I've accumulated a lot of thoughts about technology, building systems at scale, and how AI is reshaping the way we interact with information.

This blog is a place for me to share those thoughts. Expect posts about:

  • Tech deep dives — things I've learned building search and recommendation systems
  • Research reflections — papers I find interesting and why
  • Life and career — lessons from navigating academia and industry

No fixed schedule, no pressure. Just writing when I have something worth sharing.

If you want to chat about anything I write, feel free to reach out on Twitter or LinkedIn.
